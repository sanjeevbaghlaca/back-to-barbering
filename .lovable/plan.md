

# Back To Barbering – Website Plan

## Design & Style
- **Color palette**: Black, white, warm greys with gold/amber vintage barber accents
- **Typography**: Bold, clean headings with a subtle classic feel; easy-to-read body text
- **Tone**: Friendly, local, confident – not corporate
- **Fully responsive** mobile-first design

---

## Page 1: Home
- **Hero section** with large headline ("Orillia's Trusted Barber Shop – 4.7★ Rated"), subheading about great conversation and service, and two CTA buttons: "Call Now" and "Get Directions"
- **Highlights bar** showing "238+ Reviews", "Walk-ins Welcome", and "Clean & Spacious"
- **Short intro paragraph** about the shop's friendly staff, competitive pricing, and minimal wait times
- **Featured Reviews section** – 3 customer review cards with quotes like "Very well priced" and "Always a good experience"
- **Photo gallery section** with placeholder images for shop interior, haircuts, and atmosphere

## Page 2: About Us
- Story-style narrative sections covering: friendly professional barbers, clean bright decor, fast service for those in a rush, relaxed environment for those who want to chat
- Sprinkled customer praise quotes throughout

## Page 3: Services
- Clean card or list layout for: Men's Haircut, Beard Trim, Shave, Kids Haircut, Senior Haircut, Line Up / Trim
- Note about walk-ins and competitive pricing
- Prominent "Call Now" CTA with phone number

## Page 4: Contact
- Full address, click-to-call phone link, business hours
- Embedded Google Map iframe
- Simple contact form (name, email/phone, message)
- "Popular Times" note mentioning Wednesdays are busier mid-day

## Shared Components
- **Navigation bar** with logo/business name and links to all 4 pages, mobile hamburger menu
- **Footer** with address, phone, hours, and a small tagline
